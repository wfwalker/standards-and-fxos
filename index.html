the power of open standards

  • competing implementations
  • transferrable skills
  • mashups
when browsers agree on standards, they compete on performance
when developers learn standards, those skills go with them
when websites follow standards and make API's, developers can mash them up
examples: google maps, MDN, emscripten

the mobile status quo

  • closed networks and devices
  • nontransferrable skills
  • reinvent and reimplement
while we were winning the desktop, smartphone makers started it all over again 
cellphone OS's fully proprietary or driven by a single org
mobile developers segmented and pigeonholed
app makers obliged to reinvent the wheel
